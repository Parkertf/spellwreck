# Spellwreck — Rules & FAQ

## Setup

1. Open the app on a TV or large screen
2. Add player names — you need at least 1 player
3. Choose your word sets (Easy, Medium, Hard, Kids, or themed sets like The Office, Harry Potter, etc.)
4. Set a timer duration or turn it off
5. Choose whether the timer auto-starts or requires a manual tap
6. Toggle Endless mode if you want to play without running out of words
7. Toggle Sound on or off
8. Hit **Start Game**

## How to Play

1. The speller stands up and turns their back to the screen
2. The screen shows hangman-style blanks (one per letter)
3. When the speller is ready, someone clicks the blanks or presses any key to reveal the word
4. The audience reads the word aloud — do NOT spell it
5. The speller attempts to spell the word out loud, letter by letter
6. The host taps one of three buttons:
   - **Correct** — the speller nailed it (+1 point)
   - **Wrong** — the speller got it wrong (0 points, word stays in the pool for a future round)
   - **Skip** — pass on the word (0 points, word stays in the pool)
7. Play passes to the next player

## Rounds

- A **round** is one turn per player
- Each player gets a different word per turn
- Words answered **Correct** are removed from the pool permanently
- Words answered **Wrong** are removed for the current round but come back in the next round so someone else gets a shot
- Words that are **Skipped** stay in the pool and may appear again immediately

## Scoring

- +1 point for each correct answer
- 0 points for wrong or skipped
- Scores are shown in the scoreboard panel (tap the menu icon in the top-left)

## Streaks

- Spelling 3 or more words correctly in a row earns a streak badge
- Streaks reset on a wrong answer or skip
- Best streaks are shown in the end-of-game summary

## Timer

- When enabled, a countdown bar appears after the word is revealed
- If the timer runs out, it counts as a wrong answer
- The timer can be paused/resumed by tapping the play/pause icon next to the bar or pressing **Spacebar**
- If auto-start is off, the host must tap the play icon to begin the countdown

## Fullscreen Mode

- Tap the fullscreen icon in the bottom-left corner for a distraction-free view
- Shows only the player name, the word, the timer bar, and Correct/Wrong/Skip buttons
- Click the background or press **Escape** to exit fullscreen

## Keyboard Shortcuts

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| `1` or `Left Arrow` | Correct |
| `2` or `Down Arrow` | Wrong |
| `3` or `Right Arrow` | Skip |
| `Spacebar` | Pause / resume timer |
| `Escape` | Exit fullscreen |
| Any key | Reveal word (when blanks are showing) |

## Endless Mode

When enabled, the word pool automatically resets when all words have been used. Scores and streaks carry over. The game continues until someone hits **End Game** from the gear menu.

## End Game

Tap the gear icon (top-right) and select **End Game** to see:

- **Podium** — final standings with medals
- **Best Streaks** — longest consecutive correct answers per player
- **Most Missed Words** — every word someone got wrong

## FAQ

**Can I play solo?**
Yes. Add one player and play against your own score.

**Can I mix word sets?**
Yes. Check as many sets as you want — Easy + The Office + Pokemon, etc. Duplicate words across sets only appear once.

**What's the difference between New Game and End Game?**
- **New Game** resets scores, streaks, and the word pool but stays on the game screen
- **End Game** shows the summary screen with final results, then you can go back to setup

**Do I need internet to play?**
Only to load the page initially. Once loaded, everything runs locally in the browser with no server calls.

**Does it save my progress?**
Player names persist in your browser's localStorage. Scores and seen words reset each game.
